Please click on the Find a Team/Members page to help us connect you with other teams or members. We will only share your email with your permission with people looking for a team. Your contact information is removed upon request or after a connection is made. You will then have a way to connect and see if it is a match! Our goal is to create a stronger and sustainable FIRST Robotics community by connecting students looking to join FIRST Robotics teams with potential teams and provide a place for FIRST teams to announce they need members. RoboTeamFinder.com is a service of FTC Team 14195 Cerulean Centaurs and R&D Robotics Education, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. We are not affiliated with FIRST.
